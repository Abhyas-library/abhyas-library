Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Abhyas Library:

What are your opening hours?

We're open from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM every day, including weekends .

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you can bring your own food and drinks, but we request you to maintain cleanliness.

Is WiFi available for all members?

Yes, high-speed WiFi is available for all members. The password is provided after registration.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, PayTM), and bank transfers for membership fees.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my membership?

No

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have limited two-wheeler parking space available on a first-come, first-served basis.
